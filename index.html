About Us:-

Laundry Mart is a modern self-service laundry facility designed to provide customers with a fast, convenient, and affordable washing experience. Equipped with high-efficiency washers and dryers, it ensures clothes are cleaned thoroughly while using less water and energy. The environment is clean, well-lit, and spacious, making it comfortable for customers to wait while their laundry is processed. Friendly staff are available to assist with machine operation and provide additional services such as ironing and folding. Laundry Mart operates with flexible hours, allowing busy individuals to manage their laundry at their own convenience. With reliable machines and excellent customer service, it has become a trusted choice for households, students, and working professionals in the community.

  • Full-service wash, dry, and fold

  • Stain removal and fabric treatment
  • Express or same-day laundry service
  • Dry cleaning for delicate or specialty garments
  • Pickup and delivery service
